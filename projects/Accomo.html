ACCOMMODATION

Feel like home as Villa Crisanta garden resort offers cozy and spacious rooms for couples, families, friends and companies. You may avail of the fully-furnished and elegant main house or stay in our premium and deluxe rooms where you can relax and fall into a deep sleep amidst the sounds of nature.



**All of the rooms below are air-conditioned and come with televisions, mini-refrigerators, private bath and toilet.


**All of the deluxe rooms below come with two Queen-sized beds, televisions, electric fans and private toilet and bath.

THE MAIN HOUSE

The Main House is a superb place to relax and spend time with your family and your friends. It can accommodate up to 10 guests or persons, so what are you waiting for?

This simple and quintessential main house has two fully air-conditioned bedrooms with 2 queen-sized beds on each bedroom, a cozy and comfortable living room, dining area and a kitchen.

A private swimming pool and jacuzzi are also available for the guests.
Wake up every morning with a beautiful view and a cup of coffee!
