GREAT FOR WEDDINGS
Soon-to-be wed couples toying with the idea of destination weddings should consider Villa Crisanta.

The main pavilion has a winding staircase which is perfect for dramatic entrances and romantic pre-nuptials. With its refreshing lanai, it can accommodate 150 guests and its fully air conditioned function hall can comfortably host 300.

The room suites on the second floor may be used by the entire wedding entourage.

15 minutes from the Rosario Church and ample parking space for 250 vehicles.
